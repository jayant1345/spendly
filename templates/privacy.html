Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: your name and email address, provided at registration.
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, descriptions, and dates you enter manually.
  • Usage data: basic server logs such as request timestamps and error traces, used solely for maintaining the service.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data from your device beyond what you explicitly enter.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to operate Spendly for you:

  • To authenticate your account and keep your session secure.
  • To display your expense history and spending breakdowns on your dashboard.
  • To allow you to filter, edit, and delete your own records.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond providing the service.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored in a secured database on our server. Passwords are never stored in plain text — they are hashed using industry-standard algorithms before being saved. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data will be permanently removed from our systems.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal or financial data with any third-party services. We use Google Fonts to load typefaces for the interface; this request is made from your browser to Google's servers and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. No expense or account data is included in this request.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you at any time. You can edit your profile information from within the app, or contact us to request full account deletion.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out via the contact information on our website. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.
